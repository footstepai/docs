---
title: 'search_places'
description: 'Find places and points of interest near a location'
---

Search for places by category or keyword near a location. Unlike `geocode` (which finds specific addresses and named places), this is designed for discovery queries like "coffee shops near me" or "restaurants in Soho". Results are ranked by proximity.

## Example prompts

- "Find coffee shops near Kings Cross"
- "What restaurants are within 1km of my hotel?"
- "Are there any supermarkets near 51.53, -0.12?"
- "Show me parks within walking distance of Tower Bridge"

## What you get back

A list of matching places, each with coordinates, name, full label, distance from the search point in meters, and a confidence score. Results are sorted by proximity.
